Why should you visit us.

The luxurious Bahar Beach house situated in Kotatattu of Udupi district is a beautiful holiday home featuring a lush green garden and a balcony to witness the majestic view of the ocean. This 4 acre property comes with the access to a mesmerizing private beach and an inner courtyard view. Enjoy barbeque for dinner with your family and friends on the spacious terrace or at the furnished dinning areas. Enjoy coastal home food and chillax by the fire place. Relax comfortably in our master bedrooms and sofa beds in the halls. Your safety is our priority
